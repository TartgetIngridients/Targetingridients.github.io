About Target Ingredients

Your Trusted Partner in Animal Nutrition

Our Story

Target Ingredients Limited is a recognized leader in the distribution of premium veterinary, food, feed-raw materials and additives. Established with a clear vision and unwavering dedication to excellence, our company has rapidly emerged as a trusted partner for clients across the veterinary, food and feed industries.

With a comprehensive portfolio of high-quality products and a commitment to sustainability and innovation, we are proud to contribute to the advancement of animal nutrition and welfare worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide premium veterinary, food, feed-raw materials and additives, ensuring safety, efficacy and sustainability.

Our Vision

To be the trusted partner of choice in advancing animal nutrition, fostering sustainability, and driving positive change globally.

Customer Centric
